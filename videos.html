videos

skateboarding is my biggest source of inspiration, and by extension so are skate videos. videos like polar's "i like it here inside my mind, don't wake me next time" opened my eyes, showing me what a skate video could be. but what really kickstarted my video making however was a group from my hometown called one8t, seeing people from my local area make fully-fledged skate videos made me realise i could too.

so i brought a video camera and a fish eye lens then got my friends to skate. first making short videos of one skate session, and now making longer videos documenting the best of all of our skating over the summer.
