screen prints

after seeing the process of screen printing on instagram, i was hooked. the entire process facinates me, it's stressful, fustrating, but oh so satisfying when you get the perfect print. there's nothing quite like seeing something you've designed and printed by hand on a tshirt.

my entire setup is diy, after seeing the extorsinate prices of commertial screen printing equiptment i decided to make it myself.

i made a exposure unit using uv led strips and an audiono that exposes a screen in 30s, a very compenative time, and a 4 colour screen printing press out of wood and mdf that holds registration really well.

most of the printing i do is for my personal project pinup but i have done work for dereham roundtable and dereham scout group.
