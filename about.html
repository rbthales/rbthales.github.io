about

hi, i'm robert hales, an artist and designer from norfolk, england currently studying digital art at the university of kent.

i love to make stuff, i am always experimenting with new ways to create, both digitaly and physicaly. i enjoy life drawing, painting (abstract and realistic), collage, digital painting, screen printing, video editing, photography, and procedural art.

i am also a big fan of art history and galleries. i am always looking into artists and movements, getting catalogues out from the library, doing studies of their works, going to exhibitions, and learning about the context surrounding the art.

i hope you enjoy my work and if you have any questions or want to talk about working on a project you can go to my contact page.
