twitter dada poems

this project is a 21st-century re-imaging of the creation of “dada poems”, originally the poems were created by cutting up a newspaper article into individual words, shaking these words in a bag, selecting one at random, copying it down, then repeating until the bag is empty.

most of us no longer read newspapers, instead, we inform ourselves through social media - this shift in consumption brings with it new issues and questions.

in an era of information overload, echo chambers, and post-truth dada poems still have a lot to say. using python and pulling random words from random tweets, a dada poem is computationally generated.
