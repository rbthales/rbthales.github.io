mixed media

as well as my digital work i like to do experiments with more traditional mediums like paint, collage, and print. although i love digital art, nothing can beat the tactile feeling of creating phyisical art.

some of my biggest influences for these works are the dadaist hannah höch, who along with her peers invented photomonage as we know it today, and robert rauschenberg a 'neo-dadaist' who created works which combined painting, sculpture, and screen printing.
